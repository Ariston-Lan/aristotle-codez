In the Line at Space Mountain

In this photo it was grad nite, except it wasn't the "night" part of it yet. Anyways, me and my friends had a general plan, do as MUCH of the park during the morning so we could do the mosh pit during the night without ANY regrets.

Trust me, we DID go to that mosh pit with 0 regrets. I love this picture though, because I am not gonna lie, I didn't really appreciate how kind and amazing everyone was in this picture until I graduated. Even now I still don't. Each one of these people had been friends for years (except the blonde in the middle), which is obviously WAYYY before I showed up. The fact that they made space for me is something I will always be eternally grateful for. We did end up riding space mountain, and it was just as good as I remembered.

Truly an amazing memory, and truly amazing friends.

u, s, and ruby element

These elements each have their own respective use. U is for inline textual annotations, s is for strikethroughs, in which information is no longer relevant or accurate, and the ruby element is typically used for just assigning pronunciation for non english symbols.

Unarticulated Annotation

Primarily used for annotating inline text

The Importance of Annotating

Annotating is important to underline important details regarding all sorts of events! For example, my dog jumped out of a window! ...Okay he didn't actually, but if he did it would be important to underline it!

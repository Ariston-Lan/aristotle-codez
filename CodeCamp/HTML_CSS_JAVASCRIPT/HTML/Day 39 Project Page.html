Abbreviations

Abbreviations in HTML are pretty simple, you wrap your specific abbreviation in an "abbr" element. This is useful for distinguishing the abbreviated word from other text. You can also use the title attribute to let users know what the abbreviation stands for!

Famous Abbreviations

  • NASA stands for National Aeronautics and Space Administration

  • CIA stands for Central Intelligence Agency

  • AT&T stands for American Telephone and Telegraph

By the way, the title attribute allows you to see the abbreviations meaning by hovering your mouse over the abbreviation! Try it out!

Time

The time element is made for displaying any time or date, and the datetime attribute converts it into machine readable time!

Famous Dates
